Safety Tips

Stay safe while buying and selling on StudentHub

Meet in Public Places

Always arrange to meet buyers or sellers in well-lit, public locations on campus. Great spots include:

  • University library entrance
  • Student union building
  • Campus cafeteria or coffee shop
  • Main reception areas
üë•

Bring a Friend

Whenever possible, bring a friend or let someone know where you're going and who you're meeting. Share the details of the transaction with a trusted person.

üïê

Meet During Daylight Hours

Schedule meetups during daytime hours when there are more people around. Avoid late-night transactions, even on campus.

üîç

Inspect Before Buying

Always examine items thoroughly before completing a purchase:

  • Check electronics are working properly
  • Verify textbook editions and conditions
  • Test any functionality that's important to you
  • Compare the item to the listing photos
üí¨

Use Platform Messaging

Keep all communication within StudentHub's messaging system. This creates a record of your conversations and protects your personal contact information until you're comfortable sharing it.

üí∞

Cash Transactions

If exchanging cash:

  • Count money carefully before handing over items
  • Consider using bank transfers for larger amounts
  • Be wary of overpayment scams
  • Never send items before receiving payment

Check Reviews

Before transacting, check the other user's profile and reviews. Users with positive feedback from previous transactions are generally more trustworthy. Don't forget to leave your own reviews to help the community!

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels wrong, it probably is. Don't proceed with a transaction if you feel uncomfortable. It's okay to:

  • Cancel or postpone a meetup
  • Ask for more information or photos
  • Walk away from a deal that doesn't feel right
  • Report suspicious behaviour
