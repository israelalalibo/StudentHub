üìö What is StudentHub?

StudentHub is a peer-to-peer marketplace designed specifically for university students. It provides a safe and convenient platform for students to buy and sell textbooks, electronics, furniture, and other items they need throughout their academic journey.

Whether you're looking to save money on textbooks, find affordable electronics, or sell items you no longer need, StudentHub connects you with fellow students in your university community.

üéì Academic Project

This platform was developed as a Final Year Project for the BSc Computer Science programme at the University of Salford. It demonstrates full-stack web development skills including frontend design, backend API development, database management, and user authentication.
