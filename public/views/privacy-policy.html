Privacy Policy

How we handle your data on StudentHub

⚠️ Academic Project Disclaimer

StudentHub is a university project developed for educational purposes as part of the BSc Computer Science programme at the University of Salford. This is not a commercial platform, and no real financial transactions are processed.

1. Information We Collect

When you use StudentHub, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number (optional), and password when you create an account.
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, bio, and other details you choose to add.
  • Listing Information: Product details, images, descriptions, and pricing for items you list.
  • Communication Data: Messages exchanged between users through our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your listings to other users
  • Facilitate communication between buyers and sellers
  • Improve the platform functionality
  • Demonstrate technical capabilities for academic assessment

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform with industry-standard security practices. As this is an academic project, data may be retained for the duration of the project assessment period.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only visible to:

  • Other users (limited to your public profile and listings)
  • The project developer for maintenance and assessment purposes
  • University assessors as part of the project evaluation

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Request information about how your data is used
