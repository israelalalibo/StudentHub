## Task Scripts (Hand to Participant)

*Complete each task while thinking aloud. Share any thoughts or confusion as you go.*

**Task 1 — Account Creation (~3 min)**
Create a new account, add your first and last name, and navigate to your profile.
Done when: you are logged in and your profile shows your name.

**Task 2 — Create a Listing with AI Pricing (~5 min)**
You have a textbook to sell. Create a listing, use the AI price prediction tool (ISBN: 9780134685991), and publish the listing with at least one image.
Done when: the listing appears on the marketplace.

**Task 3 — Search and Filter (~2 min)**
Find electronics items priced under £50 using search and/or filters.
Done when: you can see at least one matching item.

**Task 4 — Contact a Seller (~1 min)**
Find any listing and send the seller a message asking about the item's condition.
Done when: the message is sent and visible in your conversations.

**Task 5 — Edit Your Listing (~2 min)**
Go to the listing you created and update its price, then save.
Done when: the new price is saved on the listing.
